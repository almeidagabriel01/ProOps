<objective>
Fix CHAT-08 auth loading bypass: LiaContainer briefly renders for free plan users during auth loading.

Purpose: The current guard `planTier !== undefined && user?.role !== "free"` evaluates to `true` when `user` is `null` (auth loading state) because `null?.role` is `undefined` and `undefined !== "free"` is `true`. This causes `LiaContainer` to flash for ALL users during the brief auth loading window, including free plan tenants.

Output: One-line fix adding `user !== null` to the guard condition.
</objective>
